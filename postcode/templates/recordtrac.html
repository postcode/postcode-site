RecordTrac

Online public records, open to everyone

RecordTrac is a new, open platform that makes it easy for agencies to communicate about public requests in a compliant, transparent, and efficient manner.

FEATURES

Open records

Next-gen open government

Anyone ‐ requestor, agency, community member ‐ can review current or past requests by keyword, status or department. This helps reduce duplicate requests.

Manage responses

Streamline the response

Staff can respond to requests through a simple interface - allowing them to respond quickly with relevant information.

Track and trace requests

Reduce fines from late responses

Track dates, requester, government point of contact, and correspondence. Sign up to receive email updates about a specific request.

Measure response

Understand response data for easy management

See total requests as well as average response time by department. Determine where to allocate resources and effort.

Make requests online

Digitize and streamline from the start

Individuals can make requests directed at specific departments and leave their contact information for follow up.
