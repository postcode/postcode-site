RecordTrac

FEATURES

Review Previous Requests

Save time for the government and public

Anyone can review current or past requests by keyword, status or department. This helps reduce duplicate requests.

Make Requests Online

Digitize and streamline from the start

Individuals can make make requests directed at specific departments and leave their contact information for follow up.

Track Requests

Reduce fines from late responses

Track dates, requester, government point of contact, and correspondence. Sign up to receive email updates about a specific request.

Manage Responses

Use a transparent and efficient process

Government employees can respond to requests through a simple interface - allowing them to respond quickly with relevant information.

Review Metrics

Understand response data for easy management

See total requests as well as average response time by department. Locate where to put resources and effort.

IN USE

Oakland, CA

RecordTrac has helped the City of Oakland process over 2,000 public requests across all city departments in its first year of use. It also helped the City save hundreds of thousands of dollars compared with their previous solution.

“The RecordTrac application is so much better than the cumbersome old system, it's really a tremendous difference. It saves me time, mostly from the far more nimble interface... the public end-users also find it much easier to work with.”

Jim Bondi
City of Oakland
